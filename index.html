About the Temple

Located in the heart of Kayastha Tola, this temple serves as a center for Santmat Satsang and meditation. It honors the life and teachings of Maharshi Santsevi Paramhans Ji Maharaj, who dedicated his life to spiritual awakening.

Important Timings

  • Morning Meditation: 5:00 AM - 6:00 AM
  • Evening Satsang: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
